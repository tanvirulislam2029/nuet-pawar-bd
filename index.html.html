About NUET

Legendary global engineering university since 1967

Our History & Legacy

Founded in 1967, Nigatola University of Engineering and Technology (NUET) has established itself as a legendary global engineering university, consistently ranked #1 in QS World University Rankings for an unprecedented 67 consecutive years.

From our humble beginnings as a technical institute, we have grown into a world-renowned center for engineering excellence, innovation, and technological advancement. Our commitment to pushing the boundaries of knowledge has made us a global leader in robotics, artificial intelligence, sustainable technology, and innovation.

Our Mission

To achieve global leadership through engineering and innovation, fostering a new generation of problem-solvers who will shape a sustainable and technologically advanced future for all.

Politics-Free Campus

Since 2024, NUET has maintained a fully politics-free campus environment, allowing students and faculty to focus entirely on academic excellence, research, and innovation without external distractions.
